How to Play

    Player One
  • "W" : barrel up
  • "S" : barrel down
  • "Q" : increase power
  • "A" : decrease power
  • "D" : fire missile
    Player Two
  • "↑" : barrel up
  • "↓" : barrel down
  • "<" : increase power
  • ">" : decrease power
  • "←" : fire missile

First Player to score a hit wins!
